Benchmarks (Net of Taxes & Fees)

Compare: what if you put the same money elsewhere? All returns adjusted for real-world deductions.
Time deposit interest rate before tax. Currently ~5-6%/yr in PH.
PH standard: 20% final withholding tax automatically deducted from bank interest. Your net rate = gross × (1 − tax%).
Expected return from stocks or REITs. Historically 8-12%/yr, higher risk.
Stock transaction tax (0.6%) + broker commissions (~0.25% each way). Applied on buy and sell. Default 1.1% round-trip.
Why deductions matter: Property returns above are already net of selling costs (CGT + broker), association dues, and property tax. For a fair comparison, bank and equity returns should also reflect real-world taxes and fees — otherwise the comparison overstates alternatives.
